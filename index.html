ABOUT US

Natural Gardens was established in the year 2006.The founders Mercy mutheki and Esther Kiboi had in mind an aim to make the world an ever green planet.The firm has distributed thousands of plants countrywide since foundation.The organization has decided to reach out to their customers using this online platform.


Our main headquaters are located at Kisii-Kisumu Ndogo,where plants are grown in large scale and nurtured by plant scientist.Our other branches are located at;

  • Along Nakuru-Nairobi Highway(Gilgil)
  • Nairobi(Kitengela)
  • Mombasa(Shimo la Tewa)
  • Nyeri(othaya)
